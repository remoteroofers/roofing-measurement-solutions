Pricing Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Volume pricing is automatically applied based on your monthly report count. The more reports you order, the lower your per-report cost. Discounts range from 10% (11-25 reports) to 20% (51+ reports). There's no commitment required—you only pay for what you use each month.

No hidden fees, ever. The price you see is the price you pay. There are no setup costs, monthly minimums, or cancellation fees. You only pay for the reports you order. Express and rush delivery options are available for an additional fee, clearly displayed during checkout.

If you're not completely satisfied with your first report within 30 days, we'll refund your payment in full—no questions asked. We're confident in our accuracy and quality, and we want you to feel confident too. This guarantee applies to your first order only.
